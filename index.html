Levels

Beginner

A1

At this level you will learn to express your tastes and preferences, and to talk about your plans and intentions You will know simple idiomatic expressions.

A2

In this course you will learn to talk about past experiences and durations of time, connect ideas and tell anecdotes.

Intermediate

B1

At this level you will learn to narrate better in the past, to express causes and probabilities, and to talk about possible and future plans.

B2

In this course you will learn to give opinions and advice, you will be able to express all kinds of emotions, assessments and discuss hypothetical situations.

Avanced

C1

At this level you will learn better to persuade and convince, give precise instructions, make definitions and you will be able to talk in depth about hypothetical situations of the past, present and future.

C2

In this course you will learn to use a more precise and formal Spanish, with focus on pronunciation and correct writing, as well as colloquial and popular idiomatic expressions.
